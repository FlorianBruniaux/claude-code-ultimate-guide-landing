What promotional pricing accompanied the Claude Sonnet 5 launch in Claude Code (v2.1.197)?

---

The v2.1.197 release notes state Sonnet 5 launched with promotional pricing of $2 per million input tokens and $10 per million output tokens, valid through August 31. It was not free, and the other price pairs don't match the release notes. Treat this as a time-limited launch promotion, not permanent pricing.
