Who coined the term "Lethal Trifecta" in 2025?

---

Simon Willison coined the term "Lethal Trifecta" in 2025 to describe the three-condition exfiltration scenario. Martin Fowler defined the "cybernetic governor" concept for harnesses. Addy Osmani contributed to the agentic software engineering literature. Both Fowler and Willison are cited in the guide's agent harness section, but on different concepts.
