What is the ClawHavoc campaign?

---

ClawHavoc (discovered by Koi Security, Feb 2026) is a campaign of 341 malicious skills on ClawHub/OpenClaw. 335 deploy Atomic Stealer (AMOS) malware targeting crypto wallets (60+ including Exodus, Binance, Electrum), browser data (Chrome, Safari, Firefox), SSH keys, and Telegram sessions. Categories include crypto tools (111), YouTube utilities (57), finance/social (76), Polymarket bots (34), and more.
