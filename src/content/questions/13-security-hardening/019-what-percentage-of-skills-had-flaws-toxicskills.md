According to Snyk's ToxicSkills report, what percentage of scanned AI agent skills had security flaws?

---

Snyk's ToxicSkills report (Feb 2026) scanned 3,984 skills across ClawHub and skills.sh. Results: 1,467 flawed (36.82%), 534 critical risk (13.4%), 76 confirmed malicious payloads, 10.9% with hardcoded secrets, 17.7% fetching remote content, 2.9% with remote prompt execution. 8 malicious skills were still live at time of scan.
