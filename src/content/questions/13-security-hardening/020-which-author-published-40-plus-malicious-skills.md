Which malicious author published 40+ skills in a programmatic malware campaign with clawhub typosquats?

---

zaycv is the most prolific malicious author identified by Snyk ToxicSkills, publishing 40+ malicious skills in a programmatic campaign. The skills use clawhub and clawdhub1 typosquats to impersonate the legitimate ClawHub CLI. Other confirmed malicious authors include Aslaep123 (crypto/trading skills), pepe276 (Unicode-obfuscated DAN jailbreaking), and moonshine-100rze (mixed prompt-injection + exfiltration).
